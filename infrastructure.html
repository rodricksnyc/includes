Progress Addressing the Design Elements

The hallmark of NSF INCLUDES is collaborative infrastructure. Collaborative infrastructure is the process by which organizations and institutions (1) come together with a shared vision; (2) map out mutually reinforcing activities; (3) develop goals, objectives and measures to assess their progress; (4) engage in continuous communication; and (5) advance the potential for expansion, sustainability, and scaling that would not be possible otherwise. NSF INCLUDES is embodied through the NSF INCLUDES five design elements of collaborative infrastructure. Each NSF INCLUDES project utilizes this infrastructure as a framework for accelerating change in addressing their broadening participation challenge. Download all survey summary tables
